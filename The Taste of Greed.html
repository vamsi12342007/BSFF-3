Synopsis

After the death of her disabled mother, Sage — a guarded woman haunted by hardship — clings to a mysterious dream: she will marry a man who will die and leave her wealthy. When she meets Shawn, a quiet and emotionally closed-off loner who appears well-off, she becomes convinced he is her fated path to fortune. Determined, she proposes to him — but he refuses. Over time, her persistence and vulnerability break through his defenses, and in a surprising turn, he proposes to her. Sage marries him, certain her dream is coming true. But after their quiet union, she discovers a devastating truth: Shawn has no inheritance. His wealth is either exaggerated or inaccessible — a life far from the riches she imagined. Disillusioned and emotionally trapped, Sage spirals into regret, questioning whether love, fate, or greed led her there. The Taste of Greed is a psychological drama about misguided ambition, emotional manipulation, and the tragic cost of believing in illusions.
